Eleni's path began in the classroom as a homeschool teacher and admissions advisor — but her expertise deepened in the most meaningful way possible: as a homeschool parent herself. She homeschooled two of her three children through 8th grade, navigating curriculum planning, record-keeping, and the transition back to traditional academics firsthand. She has personally guided two children through the college admissions process, with her third embarking on that journey this summer.
